About DivineFlow

DivineFlow was created to make pilgrimages safer and more organized by integrating modern technology with traditional values and local customs.

Mission

Reduce crowd-related incidents at pilgrimage sites through intelligent monitoring, timely alerts and volunteer coordination.

Vision

Every pilgrim experiences a calm, organized and spiritually fulfilling visit.

Key Challenges We Address

  • Unpredictable crowd surges during festivals
  • Limited real-time situational awareness for administrators
  • Slow communication between field volunteers & emergency services
